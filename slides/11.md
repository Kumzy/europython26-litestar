# “Composable” — what we actually mean

<div class="deflist mt-8">
  <div class="def">
    <div class="term">Isolation</div>
    <div class="desc">Each piece does one thing and doesn't reach into the others</div>
  </div>
  <div class="def">
    <div class="term">Explicit ordering</div>
    <div class="desc">You can trace what runs before and after the handler</div>
  </div>
  <div class="def">
    <div class="term">Replaceability</div>
    <div class="desc">Swap an integration or implementation without rewriting the route</div>
  </div>
  <div class="def">
    <div class="term">Narrow scope</div>
    <div class="desc">Apply work only to the routes and lifetime that need it</div>
  </div>
</div>

<p v-click class="text-center mt-5">
  <span class="gold font-extrabold text-xl">Handlers coordinate domain work.</span>
  <span class="text-lg">They do not own setup, policy, execution, or wiring.</span>
</p>
